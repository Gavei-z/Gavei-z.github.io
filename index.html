  • Research and implementation of beam tracking algorithms for B5G millimeter wave communications
    • A channel charting-based beam tracking algorithm for mmWave communications is proposed, reducing scan overhead and improving accuracy via autoencoder-driven feature mapping, mapping tables, and validated through simu lations and real-world 26 GHz communication system field tests.
